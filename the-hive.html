      La Colmena, Compañía de danza Ati-erra.

      2019

        Premiere: 05/2019. Teatro Mayor, Bogotá, Colombia.

        Composition of eight pieces of music combining electronic dance music with acousmatic aesthetics and sound design via live electronics. “The Hive Project” is a work that combines contemporary dance, acrobatics and aerial dance with technological elements such drones choreographies, surround sound diffusion (using ambisonics) and live video processing.
