      ‘Sabogal’, stereo fixed medium.

      2017

        Premiere: 11/2017. ‘Lleras’ Auditorium, University of Los Andes, Bogotá, Colombia.

        Electroacoustic piece that explores the composition of drones and landscape from an extra-musical reference. The resulting sound universe is characterized by the use of spectral transformation processes, using contrasting materials generated from recorded sound samples.
